Meta WhatsApp Business API

business.facebook.com — App ID: 5177850099107018
⚠ Card not yet added — add card to cover overage conversations
  • 1,000 free service conversations every month — resets on the 1st of each month, not a one-time allowance
  • ~$0.02–$0.05 per conversation beyond the 1,000
  • The free 1,000 applies to user-initiated (incoming) chats only
  • Business-initiated (outbound template) messages are billed separately
  • Without a card, the bot stops responding when the 1,000 limit is hit
Action (do when ready): Log in to business.facebook.com → Settings → Billing → Add a credit/debit card. No charge unless the 1,000 free conversations are exceeded.
